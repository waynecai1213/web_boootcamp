The Big Bang Theory

The Big Bang Theory has now finished its twelve-season run on CBS. The hour-long series finale aired Thursday, May 16, 2019. On August 22, 2018, CBS and Warner Bros. announced that The Big Bang Theory would be ending with its twelfth season. The twelfth season premiered with a special broadcast on Monday, September 24, 2018 and moved back to its regular Thursday night time period on September 27.
